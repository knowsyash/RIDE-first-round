About CAMPUS-CONNECT

Our Objectives

At CAMPUS-CONNECT, we are committed to solving the problems faced by college students who are searching for methods to earn within their campus environment. Our main objectives include:

  1. Facilitating Selling of Used Products: We provide a platform similar to OLX but tailored for isolated campus environments, where students can sell their used products hassle-free.
  2. Promoting Art and Craft: We offer students the opportunity to showcase their talents by selling their art and craft products, allowing them to earn recognition and income.
  3. Convenient Campus Shop Delivery: We facilitate the delivery of campus shop items to students at door steps , ensuring convenience and accessibility.
